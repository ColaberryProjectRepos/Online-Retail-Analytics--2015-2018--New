Importing Data to Power BI 8






Project Essentials Zip:


You can download the updated dataset (Online Retail Analytics) and save it on your computer from the zip file attached. The updated dataset has been modified to remove unnecessary data, including Personally Identifiable Information (PII), from the source file.

Step 1:

  1. Open Power BI Desktop
  2. Click on Get data button on the home tab in Power BI Desktop.
  3. Select Text/CSV and click on connect 
  4. Select the 'Online Retail Analytics' CSV file and click on Open
  5. A preview of the data will be displayed, click on Load.
